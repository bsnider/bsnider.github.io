Hi, I am Bradley!

Hello everybody. I'm Bradley, a passionate Detroiter, deeply engaged in the nonprofit and civic arenas. Currently, I am on a year-long adventure, living in Tucson, AZ for 2013, studying for my Master of Science in Geographic Information Systems Technology at the University of Arizona. I hope to use these newly acquired skills in GIS analysis and cartographic storytelling to continue working towards social justice.

In addition to my studies, I am also currently enhancing my skills through an internship with Pima County.   As a GIS Intern at the Pima County Community Development and Neighborhood Conservation department, I am  putting my GIS skills to good use, preparing, analyzing and mapping various socio-economic, demographic, and community infrastructure and asset datasets.   This data is submitted for federal entitlement allocation and used by the CDNC department to analyze how to best use these funds.
